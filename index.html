AT BRAINX ACADEMIA, WE EMPOWER THE YOUNG MINDS

MEET THE TEAM

BrainX Academia, a nonprofit organization, was established in January 2023 by two visionary high schoolers, Ronit Singh and Afreen Hossain. Unlike many educational initiatives that focus on specific communities, BrainX Academia is committed to holistic development for all children, regardless of their economic or social background. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and inclusive educational environment that nurtures the intellectual and personal growth of every student, empowering them to reach their full potential. Through innovative programs and dedicated mentorship, we strive to break down barriers and create opportunities for learning and development for every young mind.
