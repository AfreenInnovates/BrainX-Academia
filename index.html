about us

BrainX Academia is an avant-garde non-profit organization co-founded by Ronit Singh and Afreen Hossain, driven by a steadfast commitment to forge a platform for socioeconomically disadvantaged children.

To actualize its lofty ambitions, BrainX Academia engenders synergy through alliances with local communities, educational institutions, and impassioned volunteers. Orchestrating these strategic partnerships, the organization conceives and implements innovative programs that lay emphasis on holistic development, scholastic eminence, and individual maturation. At the epicenter of Brain X Academia's initiatives lies the invaluable concept of Education Empowerment.
