AFREEN HOSSAIN

CO-FOUNDER

Afreen Hossain is a dedicated professional known for her passion for innovation and education. Currently employed as a Project Manager at Dyne Research, Afreen excels in managing projects with precision and ensuring their successful completion. In her role, she is responsible for documenting project requirements, maintaining organizational structure, and creating strategic timelines to facilitate project progress effectively

Beyond her professional endeavors, Afreen is actively involved as a mentee in the Aerospace Mentorship Program by Women in Aerospace - Barcelona, demonstrating her commitment to personal and professional growth. Additionally, she is a Co-Founder of BrainX Academia, where she leads a team in providing educational materials and workshops to students, aiming to foster growth and holistic development.

Afreen's diverse experiences, from her involvement in the Think Tank Scientific Community to her role as a Qurios Blogger, highlight her dedication to making a positive impact in various domains. With a strong drive to create meaningful change and a relentless pursuit of knowledge, Afreen continues to inspire others in her field.

RONIT KUMAR SINGH

CO-FOUNDER

Mr. Ronit Kumar Singh is a distinguished leader in the fields of education, technology, and entrepreneurship, spearheading the vibrant community of Agragaami dedicated to nurturing the talents of the top 1% of meritorious students. With a background in Science, Technology, and Politics from Cornell University, Ronit brings a wealth of experience and expertise to his role as the president of Agragaami.

As the co-founder of BrainX Academia, a non-profit organization focused on student development, he has impacted the lives of over 1000 students globally. His commitment to empowering the next generation of leaders is evident in his visionary leadership and dedication to fostering a collaborative and inclusive environment at Agragaami.

Beyond his philanthropic endeavors, he is also an accomplished author, having published his book, "Python: Mastering the Basics and Beyond," at a young age.

KHYATI KAPUR

CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

Khyati Kapur is a dynamic individual known for her leadership and dedication to various endeavors. Currently serving as the Head Girl and President at MAPS MUN, Khyati exemplifies her passion for diplomacy and global affairs. She also holds the position of National Lead (INDIA) at Re-Defined, where she plays a pivotal role in shaping initiatives for societal impact.

With her extensive experience in public relations and business development, Khyati serves as the Public Relations Manager and Head of Business Development at HumaniTech, where she demonstrates her prowess in corporate image management and relationship building. Additionally, Khyati is a Youth Ambassador at AUNUA Global, where she actively contributes to initiatives promoting youth engagement and empowerment.

As the Chief Operating Officer at BrainX Academia, she spearheads strategic initiatives aimed at enhancing educational outreach and impact. Khyati's multifaceted skills and unwavering commitment make her a valuable asset to any organization she is a part of.
